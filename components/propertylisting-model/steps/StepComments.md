# ListProperty Step Guide

1. **PROPERTY DETAILS** – collect property name, type, rating, address, and description so the listing has essential context.
2. **ROOM CATEGORIES** – define room tiers with sequences, meal plans, and custom categories that describe how guests can book.
3. **AMENITIES** – choose from curated amenity groups (popular, wellness, outdoors, etc.) so each listing clearly shares facilities.
4. **MENU DETAILS** – document in-house food options, pricing, and inclusions for guests who rely on your dining plans.
5. **PHOTOS** – upload hero and gallery shots to showcase every living space, room category, and amenity.
6. **EXTRA SERVICES** – list complimentary and premium add-ons (spa, celebration kits, adventure) to customize each stay.
7. **TRANSFER SERVICE** – provide airport, railway, and local transfer pricing so guests can plan arrival logistics.
8. **PRICING & POLICIES** – assert pricing strategies, cancellation rules, and other commercial guardrails.
9. **UPLOAD DOCUMENTS** – attach legal, safety, and identity documents needed for verification.
10. **REVIEW** – confirm every detail before submitting the listing to go live.

> This markdown file documents every step shown inside `ListProperty.tsx` so it is easy to read without diving into the component logic.
